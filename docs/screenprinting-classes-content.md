## About the Classes

### Body
Learn the art of screenprinting in our studio — housed in the old hose-drying tower of a 1912 firehouse (yes, the one from The Princess Diaries).

Carolin has been screenprinting for [X years] and teaches small-group workshops for beginners and anyone who wants to get their hands dirty making something real.

Each class covers:
- Preparing your design for screen
- Coating and exposing screens
- Ink mixing and color theory basics
- Printing techniques — pulling clean prints every time
- Troubleshooting common issues

You'll leave with your own finished prints and the knowledge to keep going at home.
